06 Teach : Team Activity - OpenGL Environments

Overview

For the team activity today, your goal is to help your classmates (and yourself) get an environment up and running for OpenGL development.

Instructions

Today, rather than work with your traditional teams, you should find other students who are looking at the same OpenGL option that you are. Then, help each other get it working.

This activity is designed to be completed in class. Before coming, you should identify what OpenGL option you are going to choose and get the necessary files downloaded.

See here for instructions of setting up and OpenGL environment.

Completion

You should be able to run the "uiTest" program on your computer and move around the shape on the screen.

For the submission the following are the core and stretch requirements for this activity:

Core Requirements

  1. Environment of your choice is downloaded / installed.
  2. uiTest program is copied to your system.
  3. uiTest program runs on your system.

Stretch Challenges

  1. Moon lander starting code is copied over to your environment.
  2. A class is at least created for your lander class.
  3. Moon lander program starting code compiles and runs on your system.

Submission

When you have your environment up and running, answer the accompanying questions in the I-Learn quiz.

There is no instructor solution for this assignment. You may simply put "N/A" in that question box.
